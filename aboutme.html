Types of Pets

Many people own a large variety of pets for many reasons.

Dogs

One of the most popular type of pets is the dog. There are many reasons why people own dogs

  • They keep their owners company.
  • They teach their owners responsibility and empathy.

Cats

This is a link to a cat adopting website.

Cats are another popular pet among people. These are the many benefits of having a cat.

  • They are low maintenece.
  • They keep the house rodent-free.
  • Petting the cat can reduce stress.

Hamsters

For those who have little space, the hamster is the pet for you. Here are some pros of having a hamster.

  • They are inexpensive to feed.
  • They do not require special grooming.
  • They are easy to clean up after
